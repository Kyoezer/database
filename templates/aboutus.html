About Us

The Creative Bhutan is an initiative of the Royal Government of Bhutan to create a platform for the creative artists to connect and network with the local and global markets. This system maintains comprehensive data and information of the local creative practitioners, and it helps promote creative initiatives, programs and events happening in Bhutan.

Key Features

1.Registered members can show or hide their personal information in this system
2.For registration, creative practitioners have to fill out an online registration form for approval

Objectives

1.Promote the creative industry in Bhutan to diversify the economy and generate employment and income
2.Maintain comprehensive data and information of the creative artists
3.Connect creative practitioners with the local and global markets
4.Promote initiatives, programs, events, and activities related to the creative industry
